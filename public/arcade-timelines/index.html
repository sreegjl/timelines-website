    Welcome to Arcade Timelines, the exciting journey to your favorite arcade decade.

    Relive your childhood, find those long-forgotten games and enter the golden age of arcade games again!

    With one mouse click or touch screen tap you are always up-to-date with latest MAME and Gaming History information, including game images from Arcade Database.

    1. 1. Choose your favorite arcade decade (enter full screen mode with bottom right button!).
    2. 2. Decide which manufacturer spans you want to see. One-year spans (same start and end year) are displayed as a dot on the timeline.
    3. 3. Left panel -> Groups tabpage: make all groups, which contain the starting symbol of the manufacturer(s) in 2., visible. E.g. Atari => group 'a', Sega => group 's', ... .
    4. 4. Left panel -> Tags tabpage: make the desired tags visible (or keep them all visible).
    5. 5. Left panel -> Timeline tabpage: choose sorting and grouping options. Click any era (arcade era), span (arcade manufacturer) or event (arcade cabinet) to visualize it in the timeline or click it directly in the timeline itself.
    6. 6. Right panel: after executing 5., see arcade cabinet information and images, including clickable links.
